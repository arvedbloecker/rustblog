About me

I focus on understanding the core principles of programming, especially through low-level work which provides direct control over system behavior. Reliability is one of the core pillars of good software design and i prefer to resolve conflicts with a clear, deterministic approach. I believe in simplicity and languages with a strong typing system as they help to enforce correctness at compile time, reducing the need for extensive testing. I strive to find solutions that respect the hardware's constraints.
